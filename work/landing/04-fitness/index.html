Twelve weeks,
three phases

Every week has a job. You start by building a base, load through the middle, then peak into the heaviest lifts of your life.

Base — weeks 1–4 Build — weeks 5–9 Peak — weeks 10–12
01–04Base

Groove the patterns

Rebuild clean technique on the squat, press, and deadlift at moderate loads. We set your working maxes and dial in bracing and bar path.

Technique + volume
05–07Build

Load the engine

Weights climb week over week while reps stay honest. Accessory work targets the weak links that cap your main lifts.

Progressive overload
08–09Build

Push the ceiling

Intensity + deload
10–12Peak

Taper the volume, sharpen your singles, and hit the platform for new personal records. This is the week the work pays off.
