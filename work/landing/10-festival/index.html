Good to know

Festival Info

Location

Station 1, White Beach, Boracay, Aklan. 15 min from Caticlan Airport via boat + shuttle.

Stages

Shoreline Stage (main, oceanfront) and The Underground (bass tent, till late). Silent-disco after-hours nightly.

Ages

Strictly 18+. Valid government ID required at entry — no exceptions.

Getting there

Official festival shuttles from Caticlan & Kalibo. Partner hotels bookable at checkout.

Are tickets refundable?

Passes are non-refundable but fully transferable up to 7 days before the gates open. Manage yours in the app.

Is there re-entry?

Yes — your digital wristband lets you come and go all weekend across all pass tiers.

What can I bring?

Empty reusable bottles, sunscreen, and good vibes. No outside drinks, glass, or professional cameras.

Where do I stay?

Boracay has stays for every budget within walking distance. Island Club holders get priority partner-hotel rates.

Is it cashless?

100% cashless. Top up your wristband in-app or at any on-site kiosk with GCash, Maya, or card.
