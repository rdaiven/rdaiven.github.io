CapabilitiesWhat we take on

  • Product design

    Flows, screens, and states for web apps — dashboards, onboarding, checkout. We design against your real data, not placeholder rows.

  • Front-end build

    The two people who design your product also write its HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Nothing is lost in a handoff, because there isn't one.

  • Design systems

    Tokens, components, and written usage rules your in-house team can extend after we leave. Documented in the codebase, not a slide deck.

  • Brand & identity

    Wordmarks, type, and voice for companies that mostly live on screens. We only take brand work we'll also build the site for.

The studioSmall on purpose

Hilaga means "north" in Filipino. We started in 2019 in a two-room office above a printing press in Teachers Village, Quezon City, and we've stayed at five people since. Every project gets a designer, an engineer, and a producer — the same three humans from kickoff to launch.

Our clients are startup founders and marketing leads in the Philippines, Singapore, and Vietnam who need product work done properly the first time, on networks and devices their real customers actually use.
